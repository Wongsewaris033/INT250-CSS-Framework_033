Unit Conversions
Temperature



To convert celsius to fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the 'C' with your temperature in celcius: (C x 9/5) + 32
Weight


To convert kilograms to pounds yourself, use this formula replacing the 'kg' with your weight in kilograms: kg x 2.205
Distance


To convert kilometers to miles yourself, use this formula replacing the 'km' with your distance in kilometers: km รท 1.609
